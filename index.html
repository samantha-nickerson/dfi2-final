About Rubber Buzzard

Rubber Buzzard is the musical pseudonym of Jonny Parks, a 22 year old vocalist/multi instrumentalist from Ottawa, ON. In 2009-2010, he formed and fronted the rap/rock band Hot Sugar before moving to Montreal to go to university, get some culture, and find some inspiration. It was here that he began recording and performing solo material under the name Rubber Buzzard, and has since co-written and performed with emerging rapper and longtime friend, Jutes.


Aside from writing and recording music, Rubber Buzzard has also been producing artwork for a number of years. He has recently taken to the city of Toronto to pursue his musical/artistic career; trying to evoke some feelings with sounds and images.
